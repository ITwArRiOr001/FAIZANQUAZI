Faizan Quazi · User Experience Analyst

Designing meaningful experiences, evidence first.

Faizan is a UX Analyst, designer and researcher based in Pune. Across nearly a decade he has moved from independent graphic work into deep enterprise product design — currently improving B2B usability across the entire HCL Commerce software suite.

His instinct is to listen before designing: gather segmented data from the client base, map the information architecture, prototype against real personas, and prove decisions through usability testing — always shoulder-to-shoulder with product managers, engineers and clients. Like the orca, the work is intelligent, collaborative and exact.
