WHAT DRIVES ME

Research-led.
Outcome-driven.

I’m a Product Designer and User Experience Analyst at HCL Software, based in Pune. Over the past decade I’ve moved from graphic and freelance work into enterprise product design — currently improving B2B usability across the HCL Commerce suite.

My approach is straightforward: understand the user first, then design systems that respect their time and reduce friction. I work closely with product managers, engineers, and clients to turn complex requirements into clear, testable solutions.

HOW I WORK

A clear, repeatable process.

01

User Research & Interviews

Gathering segmented insights directly from the client base and end users.

02

Synthesis & Framing

Mind mapping, card sorting, flowcharts, and persona development to define clear direction.

03

Information Architecture

Structuring content and permissions so complex systems feel simple and logical.

04

Design & Validation

Wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing — iterating until the experience holds up in testing.
